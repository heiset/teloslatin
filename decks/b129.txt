Quōmodo Causa Exprimātur — Cum / Quoniam = Quoniam ita est — “Since it is so” — Cicero

Since it is raining, I take my umbrella = Cum pluit, umbellaṃ meam capiō
Since you do not understand, I get angry = Cum nōn intellegitis, īrāscor
Since it is late, I run = Cum sērō sit, currō
Since it is cold, I put on a cloak = Cum frīgora sint, paenulam induō
