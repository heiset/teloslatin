Quid (Direct Object Practice) = Quid est veritās? — “What is truth?” — Pontius Pilate

Show me the pen = Monstrāte mihi calamum
We show the pen = Calamum mōnstrāmus

Pick up the pen. What are you picking up? = Capite calamum. Quid capitis?
We pick up the pen = Calamum capimus

Put the pen on the table. What are you putting on the table? = Pōnite calamum in mēnsā. Quid pōnitis in mēnsā?
We put the pen on the table = Calamum pōnimus

Show me the book = Mōnstrāte mihi librum
The girls show the book to the woman = Puellae librum mulierī mōnstrant

Open the books. What are you opening? = Aperite librōs. Quid aperitis?
We open the books = Librōs aperimus

Close the books. What are you closing? = Claudite librōs. Quid clauditis?
We close the books = Librōs claudimus
