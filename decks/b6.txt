Quī sumus? (Who are we?) = "I am a philosopher." —Pythagoras

Who are we? = Quī sumus?
We are John and Susanna = Nōs Iōannēs et Susanna sumus
Who are you (plural)? = Quī estis?
Are you John and Susanna? = Vōs Iōannēs et Susanna estis?
Who are they (feminine)? = Quae sunt?
They are men = Illī sunt virī
They are women = Illae sunt fēminae
They are men and women = Illī sunt virī et fēminae
