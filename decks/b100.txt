Adflīctus Plūrālis (Plural Distress) = Miseria multōs afficit — “Misery affects many” — proverb
Hanc puellam videō esse adflīctam = I see this girl to be distressed
Haec est puella adflīcta = This girl is distressed
Puellae adflīctae lacrimās videō = I see the distressed girl’s tears
Misereor puellae adflīctae = I pity the distressed girl
Hōs puerōs videō esse perterritōs = I see these boys to be terrified
Hī puerī exterritī sunt = These boys are terrified
Puerōrum perterritōrum capillōs videō = I see the hair of the terrified boys
Misereor puerōrum perterritōrum = I pity the terrified boys
Hōs puerōs videō adflīctōs esse = I see these boys to be distressed
Hī sunt puerī adflīctī = These are distressed boys
Puerōrum adflīctōrum lacrimās videō = I see the tears of distressed boys
Misereor puerōrum adflīctōrum = I pity the distressed boys
