Familia Latiōr (Extended Family) = Propinquī sunt quasi pars animae — “Relatives are like part of the soul” — Cicero
Amita = aunt (father’s sister)
Patruus = uncle (father’s brother)
Avunculus = uncle (mother’s brother)
Mātertera = aunt (mother’s sister)
Patruēlis = cousin (father’s side, male)
Patruēlis = cousin (father’s side, female)
Cōnsobrīnus = cousin (mother’s side, male)
Cōnsobrīna = cousin (mother’s side, female)
