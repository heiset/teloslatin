Dīcō aliquid factūrum esse = Futura prōspice — “Look to the future” — proverb
Dīcō hodiē Albānum abesse Stephanum et Tertiam adesse = I say that today Albanus is absent but Stephanus and Tertia are present
Dīcō Stephanum crās abfutūrum esse adfutūrōs vērō Albānum et Iūliam = I say that Stephanus will be absent tomorrow but Albanus and Julia will be present
