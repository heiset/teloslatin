Review Dialogue Medley (Ōstium pulsā) = “When I review, I see one passage after another that should be erased.” —Ovid

Knock on the door. Are you knocking? = Ōstium pulsā. Pulsāsne?
I’m knocking on the door = Ōstium pulsō
Stephen knocks on the door = Stephanus ōstium pulsat

Open the door! Are you opening it? = Ōstium aperī! Aperīsne?
I’m opening the door = Ōstium aperiō
Stephen opens the door = Stephanus ōstium aperit

Enter the room. Are you entering? = Intrā in conclāve. Intrāsne?
I’m entering = Intrō
Stephen enters the room = Stephanus in conclāve intrat

Close the door! Are you closing it? = Claude ōstium! Claudisne?
I’m closing it = Claudō
Stephen closes the door = Stephanus ōstium claudit

Open the door! Are you opening it? = Aperī ōstium! Aperīsne?
I’m opening it = Aperiō
Stephen opens the door = Stephanus ōstium aperit

Go out of the room! Are you going out? = Exī ē conclāvī. Exīsne?
I’m going out = Exeō
Stephen goes out of the room = Stephanus ē conclāvī exit
