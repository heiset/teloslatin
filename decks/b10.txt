Monstrā mihi (Show me) = “Show me good medicine, and I will take it.” —Horace, xvii. I.

Show me the pen = Monstrā mihi calamum
Show / Touch / Take / Put (on the table) = Monstrā / Tangē / Cape / Pōne (in mēnsā)
This book / pen = Hunc librum / calamum
This cup / wine = Hoc poculum / vīnum
That chair / window / table = Illam sellam / fenestram / mēnsam
Show the student to me (m/f) = Monstrā discipulum / discipulam
Show the teacher to me (m/f) = Monstrā magistrum / magistram
Show the woman and the man to me = Monstrā mulierem et hominem
A wise and a foolish student = Prūdentem et stultum discipulum
A difficult and an easy road = Difficilem et facilem viam
