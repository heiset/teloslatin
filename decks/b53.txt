Dē Complēmentō Objectī = "Numerus rerum est fundamentum"—Boethius (adapted)

How many pens do you have? = Quot calamī sunt vōbīs?
Three pens are for us = Trēs calamī nōbīs sunt
How many pens do you have? = Quot calamōs habētis?
We have three pens = Trēs calamōs habēmus

Give us two pens = Date nōbīs duōs calamōs

How many books do you have? = Quot librī sunt vōbīs?
Four books are for us = Quattuor librī nōbīs sunt
How many books do you have? = Quot librōs habētis?
We have four books = Quattuor librōs habēmus

Give us two books = Date nōbīs duōs librōs
