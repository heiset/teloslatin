Tiberia et pluvia (Tiberia and the rain) UDT (106)

Tiberia does not put on a cloak but a raincoat = Tiberia nōn pallium induit sed paenulam
Tiberia does not pick up the umbrella but the bag = Tiberia nōn umbellam tollit sed bulgam
Tiberia goes out of the house = Tiberia exit domō
