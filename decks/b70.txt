Praeteritus Singularis I (Basic Singular Past Forms) = Facta manent — “Deeds remain” — Ovid

Surge! Surrēxistine? = Get up! Did you get up?
Surrēxī = I got up

Ambulā! Ambulāvistine? = Walk! Did you walk?
Ambulāvī = I walked

Cōnsiste! Cōnstitistine? = Stop! Did you stop?
Cōnstitī = I stopped

Cōnsīde! Cōnsēdistine? = Sit down! Did you sit down?
Cōnsēdī = I sat down

Herī Stephanus surrēxit, ambulāvit, cōnstitit, cōnsēdit = Yesterday Stephen got up, walked, stopped, and sat down

Curre! Cucurristine? = Run! Did you run?
Cucurrī = I ran

Exī! Exīstine? = Go out! Did you go out?
Exiī = I went out

Herī Stephanus surrēxit, cucurrit, cōnstitit, cōnsēdit = Yesterday Stephen got up, ran, stopped, and sat down

Herī Stephanus surrēxit, ambulāvit, exiit = Yesterday Stephen got up, walked, and went out
