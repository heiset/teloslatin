Tē Decet / Tē Nōn Decet (What is Fitting / Not Fitting) = Decet hominem honeste vivere — “It befits a man to live honorably” — Cicero
Tē decet aquam bibere = It is fitting for you to drink water
Tē decet currere cotīdiē = It is fitting for you to run daily
Tē decet holera edere = It is fitting for you to eat vegetables
Tē decet noctū dormīre = It is fitting for you to sleep at night
Tē nōn decet fūmum haurīre = It is not fitting for you to smoke
Tē nōn decet vīnum bibere = It is not fitting for you to drink wine
Tē nōn decet dulcia edere = It is not fitting for you to eat sweets
Tē nōn decet noctū lūdere = It is not fitting for you to play at night
