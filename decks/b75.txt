Dē Multiplicibus Locīs (Complex Locations) = Omnia in locō suō — “Everything in its place” — proverb

Iuxtā arcam sum = I am next to the box
Super arcam sum = I am on top of the box

Post arcam sum = I am behind the box
Inter arcās sum = I am between the boxes
Prō arcā sum = I am in front of the box
