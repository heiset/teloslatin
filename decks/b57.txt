Illōs, Illās, Illum (Those — Direct Objects) = Illī sunt nōbīs — “Those men belong to us” — classical-style phrasing

Show me the students. Whom are you showing? = Mōnstrāte mihi discipulōs. Quōs mōnstrātis?
We show those boys = Illōs mōnstrāmus
The boys show those students to the teacher = Puerī illōs discipulōs magistrō mōnstrant

Show me the girls. Whom are you showing? = Mōnstrāte mihi discipulās. Quās mōnstrātis?
We show those girls = Illās mōnstrāmus
The boys show those girls to the teacher = Puerī illās discipulās magistrō mōnstrant

Show me the teacher. Whom are you showing? = Mōnstrāte mihi magistrum. Quem mōnstrātis?
We show that teacher = Illum mōnstrāmus
The boys show that teacher = Puerī illum magistrum mōnstrant
