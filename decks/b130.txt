Affectūs (Emotions) = Omnia vincit amor — “Love conquers all” — Virgil

I am offended, I cry = Offendor, plōrō
I regret it, I ask forgiveness = Mē paenitet, veniam postulō
I am ashamed, I am silent = Mē pudet, sileō
I pity him, I visit him = Miserēor eius, eum vīsītō
I boast, I smile = Glōrior, subrīdeō
I envy that girl, I am sad = Illī puellae invidēo, maesta sum
