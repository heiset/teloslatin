Estne (Is it...?) = "Is it to be emperor? Is this your goal?" —Augustine
Estne līber in bulgā? = Is the book in the bag?
Est! = It is!
Certē! = Certainly!
Minimē! = No!
Nequāquam! = Not at all!
Nonnē est calamus in bulgā? = Isn’t the pen in the bag?
Ita est! = Yes, it is!
Est, certē! = It is, certainly!
Num magistra es? = You aren’t the teacher, are you?
Nōn sum = I am not
Estne calamus illīc? = Is the pen over there?
Estne līber illīc? = Is the book over there?
Calamī sunt hīc. = The pens are here
Lībrī sunt hīc. = The books are here
Da mihi hunc calamum = Give me this pen
Da mihi hunc librum = Give me this book
Videō mēnsam = I see the table
Videō fēlem = I see the cat
Ubī est illa mēnsa? = Where is that table?
Ubī est ille fēlēs? = Where is that cat?
Illīc est! = It is there!
Hīc est! = It is here!
