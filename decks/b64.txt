Cūr? (Cause) = Nihil sine causā — “Nothing without a cause” — Seneca

Why is Quintus tired? = Cūr Quintus fessus est?
Because he has devoted much time to studies = Quia iam multum tempus litterīs operam dat

Why is Quintus thirsty? = Cūr Quintus sitit?
Because he has been walking in the sand a long time = Quia iam multum tempus in arēnīs ambulat

Why does the teacher get angry? = Cūr magister īrāscitur?
Because the student did not finish the test = Quia discipulus probationem nōn expedīvit

Why does Marcia rejoice? = Cūr Marcia gaudet?
Because she devoted much time to the test and completed it fully = Quia multum tempus probationī operam dedit et eam plānē expedīvit
