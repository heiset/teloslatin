Hōrae (Telling Time) = Omnia tempus habent — “All things have their time” — Ecclesiastes

What hour is it? = Quota hōra est
It is the first hour = Hōra prīma est
It is the second hour = Hōra secunda est
It is the third hour = Hōra tertia est
It is the fourth hour = Hōra quarta est
It is the fifth hour = Hōra quīnta est
It is the sixth hour = Hōra sexta est
It is the seventh hour = Hōra septima est
It is the ninth hour = Hōra nōna est
It is the tenth hour = Hōra decima est
It is the eleventh hour = Hōra ūndecima est
Midday = Merīdiēs
Midnight = Media nox
It is the ninth hour of the day = Nōna hōra diēī est
It is the ninth hour after midday = Nōna hōra est post merīdiem
