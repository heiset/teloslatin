Nōndum (Not Yet — Exīre & Ōstia) = Tempus fugit — “Time flies” — Virgil

Go out = Exīte
Are you going out? = Exītisne?
We are going out = Exīmus
The boys go out = Puerī exeunt

Close the doors = Claudite ōstia
What are you closing? = Quid clauditis?
They close the doors = Ōstia claudunt

Knock on the doors = Pulsāte ōstia
What are you knocking? = Quid pulsātis?
We knock on the doors = Ōstia pulsāmus

Open the doors = Aperite ōstia
What are you opening? = Quid aperītis?
They open the doors = Ōstia aperiunt
We open the doors = Ōstia aperīmus

Enter. Are you not entering yet? = Intrāte. Nōndum intrātis?
We are entering = Intrāmus
They enter = Intrant
