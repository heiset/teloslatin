What section is this? = Introductions and little commands

This is Alexander = Hic est Alexandēr.
This is Rosa = Haec est Rōsa.

Hello (sing./pl.) = Salvē. / Salvēte.
Goodbye (sing./pl.) = Valē. / Valēte.

What is your name? = Quid tibi nōmen est?
My name is Rosa = Nōmen mihi Rōsa est.

How are you? = Ut valēs?  Ut valētis?
I am well = Valeō.

I am excellent, good, fine, well enough, not bad, bad, very bad = Optime, bene, rēctē, satis bene, nōn male, male, pessimē... valeō.

I am Trevor = Ego sum Trevor.
Who? (masc./fem.) = Quis? / Quae?

Stand up = Surge.
Walk = Ambula.
Stop = Consiste.
Sit down = Consīde.
Run = Curre.
Go out = Exī.
Jump = Salī.

Where are you / where am I? = Ubi es / sum?
Here / There = Hīc / Illīc.
Is it Trevor? = Estne Trevor?
Yes / No = Ita / Nōn.

Either Trevor or Updike? = Utrum Trevor an Updike?
Neither Trevor nor Updike = Neque Trevor neque Updike.
