Imperātīvus et Iussīvus (Commands and Jussive) = Imperāre sibi maximum imperium est — “To command oneself is the greatest command” — Seneca

Albanus rings the doorbell = Albānus tintinnābulum pulsat
Let him go away! = Abeat
Let him go away! Let him not open the door! = Abeat! Nē iānuam aperiat
Let us not do this; let him not go away = Nē hoc faciāmus, nē abeat
Albanus does not go away but enters the house = Albānus nōn abit sed domum intrat
