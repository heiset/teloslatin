Licet, Sinere (Permission) = Licet omnibus — “It is permitted to all” — classical-style

It is not permitted to cross the railway / Do not cross the railway = Nōn licet ferrīvīam trānsīre / Nōlīte ferrīvīam trānsīre

It is permitted to cross the road = Licet viam trānsīre

May I enter? = Licetne mihi intrāre?
I allow you to enter = Tē sinō intrāre

May we open the book? = Licetne nōbīs librum aperīre?
I allow you (pl.) to open the book = Vōs sinō librum aperīre
