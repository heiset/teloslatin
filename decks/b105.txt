Lac et Ebrietās (Milk and Drunkenness) = Ebriētās est voluntāria insānia — “Drunkenness is voluntary madness” — Seneca
Puella in mēnsam lāc effūsūra est = The girl is about to pour milk onto the table
Puella lāc effundit et ērubēscit = The girl pours the milk and blushes
Nicolāus bibitūrus est = Nicholas is about to drink
Nicolāus ēbrius est = Nicholas is drunk
Germānī cerevisiam bibitūrī sunt = The Germans are about to drink beer
Germānī cerevisiam bibunt = The Germans drink beer
