Tempus cum Partibus (Time with Fractions) = Tempus fugit — “Time flies” — Virgil

It is the sixth hour and a half = Hōra sexta cum dīmidia est
It is the twelfth hour and a quarter = Hōra duodecima cum quadrante est
It is the fifth hour with three-quarters = Hōra quīnta cum dōdrante est
It is the seventh hour with a third = Hōra septima cum triente est
