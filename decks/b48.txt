Tangite (Corpus) = "Manus manum lavat"—Proverb

Touch your hand = Tangite manum
What do you touch? = Quid tangitis?
We touch hand = Manum tangimus
The boys touch hand = Puerī manum tangunt

Touch your nose / teeth / head = Tangite nāsum / dentēs / caput
They touch nose / teeth / head = Nāsum / Dentēs / Caput tangunt
