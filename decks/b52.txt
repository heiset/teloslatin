Clāmāte et Loquiminī = "Vox populī, vox deī"—Proverb

Open your mouth = Aperite ōs
They open mouth = Ōs aperiunt

Shout = Clāmāte
Do you shout? = Clāmātisne?
We shout = Clāmāmus
They shout = Clāmant

Speak = Loquiminī
We speak = Loquimur
Speak in English / French / Greek = Loquiminī Anglicē / Gallicē / Graecē
They speak in English / French / Greek = Anglicē / Gallicē / Graecē loquuntur
