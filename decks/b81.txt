Quālitātēs (Qualities / Opposites) = Virtus in mediō stat — “Virtue stands in the middle” — Aristotle
Facilis via = An easy road
Difficilis via = A difficult road
Doctus homō = A learned (wise) man
Stultus homō = A foolish man
Prūdēns discipula = A prudent (wise) student (f.)
Piger discipulus = A lazy student (m.)
Magnus vir = A big man
Parvus vir = A small man
Pulchra mulier = A beautiful woman
Foeda mulier = An ugly woman
Bonus puer = A good boy
Malus puer = A bad boy
Vir sapiēns = A wise man
Vir insānus = A mad man
Laetus puer = A happy boy
Trīstis puer = A sad boy
