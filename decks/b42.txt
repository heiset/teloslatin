Familiā Neptis — Agunt et Stant = Prima societās in ipsā coniugatione est — “The first partnership is in marriage itself” — Cicero

This is my family = Haec est familia mea

We all stand on our feet; nobody sits = Omnēs pedibus stāmus, nēmō sedet

Each person is smiling = Ūnusquisque subrīdet

He/she smiles = Subrīdet
He/she does not smile = Nōn subrīdet

He/she stands on his/her feet = Stat pedibus
He/she sits = Sedet

Some of us have our hands behind our backs = Nōnnūllī manūs post tergum habēmus

Some have their hands behind their backs, not all = Nōnnūllī manūs post tergum habent, nōn omnēs

Servius and Julia have their hands behind their backs = Servius et Iūlia manūs post tergum habent
