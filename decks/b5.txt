Unde oriundus? (Where are you from?) = "He who dwells everywhere, Maximus, dwells nowhere." —Martial

Where do you live? = Ubi habitās?
I live in a town = In oppidō habitō.
Where are you (all) from? = Unde oriundus (m.) / oriunda (f.) es? / estis?
I’m from... = Oriundus (m.) / oriunda (f.) sum ex...
Where were you born? = Ubi nātus (m.) / nāta (f.) es?
I was born in... = Nātus (m.) / nāta (f.) sum in...
