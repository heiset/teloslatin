Prīmum, Deinde (First, Then) = "Tempora mutantur, et nōs mutāmur in illīs"—Ovid

First, I get up = Prīmum surgō
Then, I walk / stop / sit = Deinde ambulō / cōnsistō / cōnsīdō
What do you do first? = Quid prīmum facis?
First, I get up = Prīmum surgō
What do you do next? = Quid facis deinde?
Then, I walk = Deinde ambulō
Then, I stop and sit = Deinde cōnsistō et cōnsīdō
First, I knock on the door = Prīmum pulsō iānuam
Then, I wait for the teacher’s voice = Deinde exspectō vocem magistrī
Come in! = Intrā!
Then, I open the door / enter = Deinde iānuam aperiō / intrō
Do you open the door first? = Aperīsne iānuam prīmum?
No, first I knock… then wait… then open = Nōn, prīmum pulsō, deinde exspectō, deinde aperiō
