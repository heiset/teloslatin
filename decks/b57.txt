Cūr? (Causae) = "Cūr ita facis?"—Common Latin

Why is Marcia happy? = Cūr Marcia gaudet?
Because she got a perfect score = Quia probationem perfectam dedit

Why is Quintus tired? = Cūr Quintus fessus est?
Because he worked a long time = Quia iam multum tempus operam dedit

Why is Quintus sitting? = Cūr Quintus sedet?
Because he walked a long time in the sand = Quia iam multum tempus in arenā ambulāvit

Why is the teacher angry? = Cūr magister īrāscitur?
Because the student did not hand in the test = Quia discipulus probationem nōn expēdīvit
