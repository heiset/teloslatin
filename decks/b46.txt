Imperātīva (Corpus Movēte) = "Mens sana in corpore sano"—Juvenal

Stretch your fingers = Extendite digitōs
Contract your fingers = Contrahite digitōs
What do you stretch / contract? = Quid extenditis / contrahitis?
We stretch / contract fingers = Digitōs extendimus / contrahimus
They stretch / contract fingers = Digitōs extendunt / contrahunt

Stretch your arms = Extendite bracchia
Contract your arms = Contrahite bracchia
What do you stretch / contract? = Quid extenditis / contrahitis?
We stretch / contract arms = Bracchia extendimus / contrahimus
They stretch / contract arms = Bracchia extendunt / contrahunt
