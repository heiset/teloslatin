Nōmina et Ordō (Names and Order) = Nōmen est nōmen — “A name is a name” — Plautus

I will speak first; my name is Servius = Dīcam ego prīmus, nōmen mihi Servius
I am second; my name is Bartholomew = Ego secundus, nōmen mihi Bartholomaeus
I am third; my name is Stephanus = Ego tertius, nōmen mihi Stephanus
I am fourth; my name is Alba = Ego quarta, nōmen mihi Alba
I am fifth; my name is Tiberia = Ego quinta, nōmen mihi Tiberia
I am sixth; my name is Hannibal = Ego sextus, nōmen mihi Hannibal
I am seventh; my name is Pompeia = Ego septima, nōmen mihi Pompeia
Say your names = Dīcite nōmina vestra
I am eighth = Ego octāvus
I am ninth = Ego nōnus
I am tenth and last = Ego decima et ultima
