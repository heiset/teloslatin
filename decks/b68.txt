Dē “Quandō” et Adverbiīs Temporālibus (When/Time) = Tempus fugit — “Time flies” — Vergil

Quandō puerī ad scholam eunt? = When do the boys go to school?
Cottīdiē = Every day

Quandō Nicolaus bibit? = When does Nicholas drink?
Saepe = Often

Quandō Philippus mātūrē ad lūdum pervenit? = When does Philip arrive early?
Numquam = Never

Quandō Philippus et Alexander natant? = When do they swim?
Aliquando = Sometimes

Quandō Albānus pēnsum magistrō dat? = When does Albanus give the assignment?
Tertiō quōque diē = Every third day

Quandō discipulī litterīs operam dant? = When do students work a long time?
Tōtum diem = All day

Quandō Philippus dormit? = When does Philip sleep?
Tōtam noctem = All night

Quandō Philippus natat? = When does Philip swim?
Semel in hebdomade, fēriā secundā = Once a week, on Monday

Quandō Albānus pēnsum reddit? = When does Albanus turn in work?
Bis in hebdomade, fēriīs tertiā et sextā = Twice a week, Tuesday and Friday
