Hunc / Hanc / Hoc (Direct Object Demonstratives) = Hic et nunc — “Here and now” — classical phrase

Put the books on the table. What are you putting on the table? = Pōnite librōs in mēnsā. Quid pōnitis in mēnsā?
We put the books = Librōs pōnimus

Show me the teacher. Whom are you showing me? = Mōnstrā mihi magistrum. Quem mihi mōnstrās?
I show him to you = Hunc tibi mōnstrō

Show me the woman. Whom are you showing me? = Mōnstrā mihi mulierem. Quam mihi mōnstrās?
I show her to you = Hanc tibi mōnstrō

Show me the gift. What are you showing me? = Mōnstrā mihi dōnum. Quid mihi mōnstrās?
I show this to you = Hoc tibi mōnstrō
