Quamdiū / Quamdūdum / Ā Quō Tempore (Duration) = Tempus omnia revelat — “Time reveals all things” — Seneca

How long did Charlemagne reign? = Quamdiū rēgnāvit Carolus Magnus
He reigned fourteen years = Quattuordecim annōs rēgnāvit
For how long has he been reigning? = Quamdūdum rēgnat
He has been reigning now for the fourth year = Quartum iam annum rēgnat
From what time has Elvis Presley been dead? = Ā quō tempore mortuus est Aloisius Presley
He died forty-five years ago = Abhinc quadrāgintā et quīnque annōs mortuus est
