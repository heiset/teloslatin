Dē Qualitāte et Statū (Appearance vs Reality) = Species fallit — “Appearance deceives” — proverb
Aegrōtāre vidētur at rē vērā valet = He seems to be sick but in reality he is well
Nōn aegrōtat = He is not sick
Aegrōtāre vidētur sed valet = He seems to be sick but he is well
Dīves vidētur at rē vērā pauper est = He seems rich but in reality he is poor
Dīves est = He is rich
Dīves vidētur sed pauper est = He seems rich but he is poor
Philippus pauper est = Philippus is poor
