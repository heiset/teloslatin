Dē Gaudiō et Spē (Joy, Shame, Hope, Fear) = Spēs ultima dea — “Hope is the last goddess” — Seneca

I rejoice and sing = Laetitiā exsultō, canō
I am ashamed and silent = Ērubescō, sileō
I become timid, I do nothing = Timidus fiō, nihil faciō
I rejoice because it succeeded = Gaudeō quod successit
I become anxious and afraid = Impatiēns fiō, trepidus fiō
I hope I will get many points = Spērō mē multa pūncta impetrātūram esse
The sheep fear the wolf will come = Ovēs timent nē lupus veniat
The sheep hope the dog will come = Ovēs optant ut canis veniat
