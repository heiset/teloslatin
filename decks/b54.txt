Quam / Quem / Id (Personal Pronouns as D.O.) = Nosce tē ipsum — “Know thyself” — Socrates

Whom do you see? Do you see the woman? = Quam vidēs? Vidēsne mulierem?
I see her = Eam videō

Whom do you see? Do you see the women? = Quās vidēs? Vidēsne mulierēs?
I see them (f.) = Eās videō

Whom do you see? Do you see Nestor? = Quem vidēs? Vidēsne Nestorem?
I see him = Eum videō

Do you see Nestor and Nicolaus? Whom do you see? = Vidēsne Nestorem et Nicolāum? Quōs vidēs?
I see them (m.) = Eōs videō

Where is the cup? = Ubi est pōculum?
I do not see it = Id nōn videō

Where are the cups? = Ubi sunt pōcula?
I do not see them = Ea nōn videō
