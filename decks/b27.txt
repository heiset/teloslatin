Accusātīvus/Ablātīvus (Review)

to the garden, to the school, to the shop = Ad hortum, ad scholam, ad tabernam (accusātīvus)
into the garden, into the school, into the shop = In hortum, in scholam, in tabernam (accusātīvus)
in the garden, in the school, in the shop = In hortō, in scholā, in tabernā (ablātīvus)
out of the garden, out of the school, out of the shop = Ex hortō, ē scholā, ē tabernā (ablātīvus)
from the garden, from the school, from the shop = Ab hortō, ā scholā, ā tabernā (ablātīvus)
