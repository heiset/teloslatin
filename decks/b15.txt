Quot? (How many?) = "How many condemnations have I seen more criminal than the crimes themselves?" —Cicero

How many pens are there = Quot calāmī sunt
One = Ūnus

How many bags are there = Quot bulgae sunt
One = Ūna

How many gifts are there = Quot dōna sunt
One = Ūnum

One = Ūnus -a -um
Two = Duo duae duo
Three = Trēs tria
Four = Quattuor
Five = Quīnque
Six = Sex
Seven = Septem
Eight = Octō
Nine = Novem
Ten = Decem

How many pens and bags and gifts are there = Quot calāmī et bulgae et dōna sunt
Two pens and two bags and two gifts = Duo calāmī et duae bulgae et duo dōna
Or three pens three bags and three gifts = Aut trēs calāmī trēs bulgae et tria dōna
Or four five these are invariant = Aut quattuor quīnque hī sunt invariante
