Quōmodo Causa Exprimātur — Enim = Rēs ipsa loquitur — “The thing itself speaks” — Cicero

I take my umbrella, for it is raining = Umbellam meam capiō, pluit enim
I get angry, for you do not understand = Īrāscor, nōn enim intellegitis
I run, for it is late = Currō, sērō enim est
I put on a cloak, for it is cold = Paenulam induō, frīgora enim sunt
