Conclāvia Domūs = "Domus est ubi cor est"—Proverb

In the atrium we sit with guests = In ātriō cum hospitibus sedēmus
In the dining room we eat = In trīclīniō comedimus
In the bedroom we sleep = In cubiculō dormīmus
In the kitchen we prepare food = In culīnā cibum parāmus
In the bathroom we wash = In balneō lavāmur
In the study the doctor speaks with the patient = In cellā scrīptōriā medicus cum aegrōtō colloquitur
