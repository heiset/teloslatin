Hunc, Illam, Hoc, Illud (Demonstrative Objects) = Illud quod saepe audīmus, novum est — “That which we often hear is new” — Seneca

Show me the man. Whom are you showing me? = Mōnstrā mihi hominem. Quem mihi mōnstrās?
I show this man to you = Hunc tibi mōnstrō

Show me the woman. Whom are you showing me? = Mōnstrā mihi mulierem. Quam mihi mōnstrās?
I show that woman to you = Illam tibi mōnstrō

Show me the gifts. What are you showing me? = Mōnstrā mihi dōna. Quae mihi mōnstrās?
I show you this and that = Hoc et illud tibi mōnstrō
