Conclāvia Domūs (Rooms of the House) = Domus sua cuique est tūta — “Each man’s home is safe for him” — Cicero

We sleep in the bedroom = In cubiculō dormīmus

We wash in the bathroom = In balneō lavāmur

We eat in the dining room = In trīclīniō comedimus

We prepare food in the kitchen = Cibum in culīnā parāmus

We speak with guests in the atrium = In ātriō cum hospitibus colloquimur

The doctor speaks with the sick person in the study = Medica in cellā scrīptōriā cum aegrōtō colloquitur
