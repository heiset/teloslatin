Monstrā mihi (Show me) = “Show me good medicine, and I will take it.” —Horace, xvii. I.

Show me the pen = Monstrā mihi calamum
Touch the pen = Tangē calamum
Take the pen = Cape calamum
Put the pen on the table = Pōne calamum in mēnsā
This book / pen = Hunc librum/calamum
This cup / wine = Hoc poculum/vīnum
That chair / window / table = Illam sellam / fenestram / mēnsam
Show the student to me = Monstrā discipulum/am (m/f)
Show the teacher to me (m/f) = Monstrā magistrum/am (m/f)
Show the man to me = Monstrā mihi hominem
Show the woman to me = Monstrā mihi mulierem
A wise and a foolish student = Monstrā mihi prūdentem et stultum discipulum
A difficult and an easy road = Monstrā mihi difficilem et facilem viam
