Imperātīva Corporis (Body Commands) = Mens sana in corpore sānō — “A healthy mind in a healthy body” — Juvenal

Extend the fingers. What are you extending? = Extendite digitōs. Quid extenditis?
We extend the fingers = Digitōs extendimus
They extend the fingers = Digitōs extendunt

Contract the fingers. What are you contracting? = Contrahite digitōs. Quid contrahitis?
We / they contract the fingers = Digitōs contrahimus / contrahunt

Extend the arms. What are you extending? = Extendite bracchia. Quid extenditis?
We / they extend the arms = Bracchia extendimus / extendunt

Contract the arms. What are you contracting? = Contrahite bracchia. Quid contrahitis?
We / they contract the arms = Bracchia contrahimus / contrahunt

Raise the heads. What are you raising? = Adtollite capita. Quid adtollitis?
We / they raise the heads = Capita adtollimus / adtollunt

Lower the heads. What are you lowering? = Dēmittite capita. Quid dēmittitis?
We / they lower the heads = Capita dēmittimus / dēmittunt

Raise / lower the leg = Adtollite crūs / Dēmittite crūs
They raise / lower the leg = Crūs adtollunt / dēmittunt

Raise the hand / shoulder. Lower the shoulder = Adtollite manum / humerum. Dēmittite humerum
They raise / lower = Manum / humerum adtollunt; humerum dēmittunt

Open / close the mouth = Aperīte ōs. Claudite ōs
They open / close the mouth = Ōs aperiunt / claudunt

Open / close the eyes = Aperīte oculōs. Claudite oculōs
The men open / close the eyes = Virī oculōs aperiunt / claudunt

Touch the hand / nose / teeth / head. What are you touching? = Tangite manum / nāsum / dentēs / caput. Quid tangitis?
We / the boys touch the hand = Manum tangimus; puerī manum tangunt
They touch the nose / teeth / head = Nāsum / dentēs / caput tangunt
