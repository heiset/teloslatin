Hās / Haec / Hōs (Plural Direct Objects) = Haec olim meminisse iuvābit — “One day it will be pleasing to remember these things” — Virgil

Touch the books. What are you touching? = Tangite librōs. Quid tangitis?
We touch these books = Hōs librōs tangimus

Touch the notebooks = Tangite libellōs
They touch the notebooks = Libellōs tangunt

Touch the pens = Tangite calamōs
They touch the pens = Calamōs tangunt

Lift the tables. What are you lifting? = Tollite mēnsās. Quid tollitis?
We lift these tables = Hās mēnsās tollimus

Pick up the cups. What are you picking up? = Tollite pōcula. Quid tollitis?
We pick up these cups = Haec pōcula tollimus

Pick up the bottles. What are you picking up? = Tollite lagōenas. Quid tollitis?
We pick up these bottles = Hās lagōenas tollimus
