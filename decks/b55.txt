Tolle et Pōne = "Acta, nōn verba"—Proverb

Lift your leg = Tolle crūs tuum
I lift my leg = Crūs meum tollō
They lift their leg = Crūs suum tollunt

Lift your arm = Tollite bracchium vestrum
We lift our arm = Bracchium nostrum tollimus
They lift their arm = Bracchium suum tollunt

Put your book on the table = Pōne librum tuum in mēnsā
I put my book on the table = Librum meum in mēnsā pōnō
The girl puts her book on the table = Discipula librum suum in mēnsā pōnit

Put your books on the table = Pōnite librōs vestrōs in mēnsā
We put our books on the table = Pōnimus librōs nostrōs in mēnsā
The girls put their books on the table = Discipulae pōnunt librōs suōs in mēnsā
