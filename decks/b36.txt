Cape, Ede (Take, Eat) = "Nihil est in intellectū quod nōn prius fuerit in sensū"—Aristotle

Take the apple = Cape mālum
Do you take it? = Capisne?
I take it = Capiō
He takes the apple = Mālum capit
Eat the apple = Ede mālum
Do you eat? = Edīsne?
I eat = Edō
He eats the apple = Mālum edit
Take the cup = Cape pōculum
Drink = Bibe
I drink = Bibō
He drinks = Bibit
