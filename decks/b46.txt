Imperātīvus Plūrālis (Commands & Movement) = Festīnā lente — “Make haste slowly” — Augustus

Run! You are certainly running = Currite! Certē curritis
We run = Currimus
Who is running? Those boys are running = Quī currunt? Illī currunt

Stop! You are certainly stopping = Cōnsīstitē! Certē cōnsīstitis
We stop = Cōnsīstimūs
Who is stopping? Those boys = Quī cōnsistunt? Illī

Walk! You are certainly walking = Ambulāte! Certē ambulātis
We walk = Ambulāmus
Who walks? Those boys walk = Quī ambulant? Illī ambulant

Come here! Are you coming? = Venīte hūc! Venītisne?
We are coming = Venīmus
They come here = Veniunt hūc

Go there! You go there = Īte illūc! Illūc ītis
We go = Īmus
They go there = Eunt illūc

Return to your seats! You are certainly returning = Revertiminī in sellās vestrās! Certē revertiminī
We return = Revertimur
They return to their seats = In sellās suās revertuntur
