Quālis? (Qualitas Rerum) = Forma bonum fragile est — “Beauty is a fragile good” — Ovid

Quālis est calamus? = What is the pen like?
Calamus magnus est = The pen is big

Quālis est bulga? = What is the bag like?
Bulga parva est = The bag is small

Quāle est illud dōnum? = What is that gift like?
Illud dōnum magnum est = That gift is big

Quāle est istud dōnum? = What is that (near you) gift like?
Istud dōnum parvum est = That gift is small
