Clāmāre et Loquī (Speaking & Calling Out) = Vox populī, vox Deī — “The voice of the people is the voice of God” — medieval proverb

Open your mouth = Aperīte ōs

Shout! Are you shouting? = Clāmāte! Clāmātisne?
We shout = Clāmāmus
They shout = Clāmant

Speak! Speak in English / French / Greek = Loquiminī! Loquiminī Anglicē / Gallicē / Graecē
We speak = Loquimur
They speak in English / French / Greek = Anglicē / Gallicē / Graecē loquuntur
