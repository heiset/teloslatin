Familia (Family) = Omnium societātum nulla praestantior est quam familia — “Of all associations none is more excellent than the family” — Cicero
Ego = I
Pater = father
Māter = mother
Frāter = brother
Soror = sister
Avus = grandfather
Avia = grandmother
Nepōs = grandson / nephew
Neptis = granddaughter / niece
