Review Dialogue Medley (Cape, ede!) = “When I review, I see one passage after another that should be erased.” —Ovid

Take the apple! Are you taking it? = Cape mālum! Capisne?
I’m taking it = Capiō
Alban takes the apple = Albānus mālum capit

Eat the apple! Are you eating it? = Ede mālum! Edīsne?
I’m eating it = Edō
Alban eats the apple = Albānus mālum edit

Take the cup! Are you taking it? = Cape pōculum! Capisne?
I’m taking it = Capiō
Alban takes the cup = Albānus pōculum capit

Drink! Are you drinking? = Bibe! Bibisne?
I’m drinking = Bibō
Alban drinks = Albānus bibit
