Corpus (Membra) = "Nōscē tē ipsum"—Delphic maxim

Raise / lower your legs = Adtollite / Dēmittite crūs
They raise / lower legs = Crūs adtollunt / dēmittunt

Raise your hand = Adtollite manum
Raise / lower your shoulder = Adtollite / Dēmittite humerum
They raise hand / shoulder = Manum / Humerum adtollunt
They lower shoulder = Humerum dēmittunt

Open / close your mouth = Aperite / Claudite ōs
They open / close mouth = Ōs aperiunt / claudunt

Open / close your eyes = Aperite / Claudite oculōs
The men open / close eyes = Virī oculōs aperiunt / claudunt
