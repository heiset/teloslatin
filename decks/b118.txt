Dē Cupiditātibus et Vōtīs (Desire and Wishes) = Utinam quod vīs habeās — “If only you may have what you want” — Plautus

Let us get onto the horses! = Cōnscendāmus in equōs

They got onto the horses = In equōs cōnscendērunt

Let us play with a ball! = Pilā lūdāmus

They play with a ball = Pilā lūdunt

Let us drink! = Bibāmus

They drink = Bibunt

If only I may obtain all the points! = Utinam omnia pūncta impetrem

If only I do not fall = Utinam nē cadam

The key falls = Cadit clāvis

If only I may sleep under a tree! = Utinam sub arbore dormiam

If only you would study your letters! = Utinam litterīs studeās
