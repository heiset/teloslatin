Date et Capite = "Dō ut dēs"—Roman law

Give me the money = Date mihi aera
What do you give me? = Quid mihi datis?
We give you money = Aera tibi damus
They give money to the teacher = Aera magistrō dant

Take the money = Capite aes
What do you take? = Quid capitis?
We take money = Aera capimus
They take money = Aes capiunt

Put the money on the tables = Pōnite aera in mēnsīs
What do you put? = Quid pōnitis?
We put money = Aera pōnimus
They put money on tables = Aera in mēnsīs pōnunt
