Dē Prōpōsitō (Purpose) = Finis coronat opus — “The end crowns the work” — Ovid

I go to play = Eō lūsum
I go to sleep = Eō dormītum
They come to hear the singer = Veniunt audītum
They come to buy bread = Veniunt ut pānēs emant
He follows the girls to move them to love = Sequitur ut moveat
He drinks so that he may not die = Bibit nē moriātur
