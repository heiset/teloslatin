Placet / Nōn Placet (Likes & Dislikes) = Suum cuique placet — “Each person likes his own thing” — Cicero

Do you like wine? = Placetne tibi vīnum?
I like it very much = Valdē placet

Do you like cookies? = Placetne tibi crustula?
Not at all = Nēquāquam

Do you like chicken? = Placetne tibi pullīna?
Maybe = Fortasse

I like to speak with you = Mihi placet tēcum loquī
…but I do not like it = Sed mihi nōn placet

Do you like this picture? = Placetne tibi haec imāgō?

Do you like this clock? = Placetne tibi hoc hōrologium?

Do you like this car? = Placetne tibi haec raeda?
I like it … but I prefer that one = Placet … hanc autem mālō
