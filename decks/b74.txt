Praeteritus Plūrālis (Plural Past Forms) = Concordia rēs parvae crescunt — “Through harmony small things grow” — Sallust

Surgite! Surrēxistisne? = Get up! Did you get up?
Surrēximus = We got up

Ambulāte! Ambulāvistisne? = Walk! Did you walk?
Ambulāvimus = We walked

Cōnsīdite! Cōnsēdistisne? = Sit down! Did you sit down?
Cōnsēdimus = We sat down

Puerī surrēxērunt = The boys got up
Puerī ambulāvērunt = The boys walked
Puerī cōnsēdērunt = The boys sat down
