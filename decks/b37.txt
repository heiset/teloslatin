Prandia (Lunch) = "Vīvere est edere"—Proverb

In the morning we have breakfast = Mānē ientāculum habēmus
In the morning we eat breakfast = Mānē ientāmus
At midday we have lunch = Merīdiē prandium habēmus
At midday we eat lunch = Merīdiē prandēmus
In the evening we have dinner = Vesperī cēnam habēmus
In the evening we eat dinner = Vesperī cēnāmus
