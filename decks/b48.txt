Imperātīvus Plūrālis (Movement & Direction) = Omnēs viās Rōmam dūcunt — “All roads lead to Rome” — medieval proverb

Come to the board = Venīte ad tabulam

Come to the door = Venīte ad ōstium

Come to the window = Venīte ad fenestram

Go to the window = Īte ad fenestram
They go to the window = Ad fenestram eunt

Stand up = Surgite
They stand up = Surgunt

Follow me! Do you follow me? = Sequiminī mē! Mēne sequiminī?
We follow = Sequimur
They follow the woman = Mulierem sequuntur

Stop = Cōnsīstitē
They stop = Cōnsistunt

Turn! Are you turning? = Vertiminī! Vertiminīne?
We turn = Vertimur
They turn = Vertuntur

Turn to the left = Vertiminī ad laevam

Turn to the right = Vertiminī ad dextram
