Dē Coniectūrā (General Truths) = Usus est magister optimus — “Practice is the best teacher” — Cicero

If you study long, you get all the points = Sī diū litterīs operam dās, omnia pūncta impetrās
If you get all the points, you rejoice and laugh = Sī omnia pūncta impetrās, gaudēs et rīdēs
If someone drinks water, he does not die = Sī aquam bibit, nōn moritur
If someone walks long, he becomes tired = Sī quis diū ambulat, fessus fit
