Aperī/Claude (Open/Closed) = “Then at last truth issues from the heart; the visor’s gone, the man remains.” —Lucretius, iii.  57.

Go to the window = Ī ad fenestram
Go to the  door = Ī ad ostium
Open the window = Aperī fenestram
Close the window = Claude fenestram
Stay! = Manē!
Stand! = Stā!
Inside / Outside = Intus / Forīs
Knock on the door = Ostium pulsā
Go out of the classroom = Exī ē conclāvī!
Open your mouth and eyes = Aperī ōs et oculōs!
Close your mouth! = Claude ōs!
Go up! = Ascende!
Go down! = Dēscende!
