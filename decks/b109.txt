Volō aliquid / aliquem aliquid facere = Velle est posse — “To want is to be able” — proverb
Vult domō exīre = He wants to leave the house
Quid vult = What does he want
Vult amīcum suum domō exīre = He wants his friend to leave the house
Cūr īrāscitur = Why is she getting angry
Nōn vult fīlium suum tōtam noctem vigilāre = She does not want her son to stay awake all night
