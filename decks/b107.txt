Dē Multiplicibus Mōtibus (Complex Movements) = Ars est celāre artem — “Art is to conceal art” — proverb
Heus Nestor Būcephalum dūc in stabulum = Hey Nestor lead Bucephalus into the stable
Eum nōlī trahere sed dūc = Do not drag him but lead him
Nestor Būcephalum nōn dūcit sed eum trahit = Nestor is not leading Bucephalus but dragging him
Bulgam fer ego autem umbellam ferō = Carry the bag I however carry the umbrella
Ad lūdum tē dūcō = I lead you to school
Quid facis mamma = What are you doing mom
Māter fīliam ad lūdum dūcit = The mother leads her daughter to school
Iace nōbīs clāvēs = Throw us the keys
Accipe eam = Catch it
Iace mihi pilam = Throw me the ball
