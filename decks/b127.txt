Quōmodo Causa Exprimātur — Igitur = Ergo factum est — “Therefore it happened” — Cicero

It is raining; therefore I take my umbrella = Pluit, umbellaṃ meam igitur capiō
You do not understand; therefore I get angry = Nōn intellegitis, īrāscor igitur
It is late; therefore I run = Sērō est, currō igitur
It is cold; therefore I put on a cloak = Frīgora sunt, paenulam igitur induō
