Licet et Cūr = "Quid licet Iovī, nōn licet bovī"—Proverb

It is not permitted to cross the railway = Nōn licet ferrī viam transīre
It is permitted to cross the road = Licet viam transīre

May I enter? = Licetne mihi intrāre?
Yes, enter = Tē sinō intrāre

We allow you to open the book = Vōs sinō librum aperīre
May we open the book? = Licetne nōbīs librum aperīre?
