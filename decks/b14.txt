Dā mihi (Give me) = “O Varus! give me back my legions!” —Augustus Caesar

Give me...! = Dā mihi...!
Give me... (if the thing you want is plural)! = Dāte mihi!
Give us = Dā nōbīs
Give them = Dā eīs
I like (it pleases me) = Mihi placet
You like = Tibi placet
He / She likes = Eī placet
We like = Nōbīs placet
They like = Eīs placet
