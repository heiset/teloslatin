Cuius Colōris? (Colors) = Color omnibus est commune bonum — “Color is a common good to all” — Seneca
Cuius colōris est caelum? = What color is the sky?
Caelum caeruleum est = The sky is blue
Cuius colōris est stella? = What color is the star?
Stella flāva est = The star is yellow
Cuius colōris est rosa? = What color is the rose?
Rosa alba est = The rose is white
Cuius colōris est mālum? = What color is the apple?
Mālum rubrum est = The apple is red
Cuius colōris est rāna? = What color is the frog?
Rāna viridis est = The frog is green
Cuius colōris est equus? = What color is the horse?
Equus niger est = The horse is black
