Dē Officiīs et Opportunitāte (Obligation and Convenience) = Officium est quod faciendum est — “Duty is that which must be done” — Cicero
Oportet hunc discipulum pēnsīs operam dare in lūmine = This student ought to give attention to his tasks in the light
Nōn oportet pēnsa facere sine lūmine = One ought not to do work without light
In lūdō bene discendum est = At school, one must learn well
In lūdō enim nōn dormiendum est = For at school, one must not sleep
