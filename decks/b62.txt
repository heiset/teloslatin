Quis? Quem? Cui? Quibus? (Cases & Roles) = Homo sum — “I am a human being” — Terence

Hello = Salvē

Who greets? = Quis salūtat?
The teacher greets = Magister salūtat

Whom does he greet? = Quem salūtat?
The teacher greets the student = Magister discipulum salūtat

Give me your book = Dā mihi librum tuum

To whom does the student give the book? = Cui discipulus librum dat?
He gives the book to the teacher = Magistrō librum dat

Take the coin = Capite aes

To whom does the teacher give the coin? = Quibus magister aes dat?
To the students = Discipulīs
