Vidētur (It Seems) = Vidētur quod est — “It seems what it is” — adapted
Mihi pulchra vidētur = It seems beautiful to me
Quālis stola mea tibi vidētur = How does my dress seem to you
Ubi est māter = Where is mother
In hortō est ut mihi quidem vidētur = She is in the garden as it seems to me
Māter mihi vidētur in hortō esse = Mother seems to me to be in the garden
Quid nunc facit Quīntus = What is Quintus doing now
Litterīs operam dat ut mihi quidem vidētur = He is working on his studies it seems to me
Quīntus mihi vidētur litterīs operam dare = Quintus seems to me to be working on his studies
