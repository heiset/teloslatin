Salūtātiō et Dātīvus = "Salvēte, amīcī"—Common Latin

Hello = Salvē
The teacher greets = Magister salūtat
Who greets? = Quis salūtat?

The teacher greets the student = Magister discipulum salūtat
Whom does he greet? = Quem salūtat?

Give me your book = Dā mihi librum tuum
The student gives a book to the teacher = Discipulus magistrō librum dat
To whom does he give the book? = Cui discipulus librum dat?

Take the money = Capite aes
To whom does the teacher give money? = Quibus magister aes dat?
To the students = Discipulīs
