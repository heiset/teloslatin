Dīcō… Quaerō utrum… an… = Verba volant — “Words fly” — proverb
Dīcō Albānum abesse Stephanum autem adesse = I say that Albanus is absent but Stephanus is present
Discipulus numerum quaerit = The student asks for the number
Discipulus ex magistrō numerum quaerit = The student asks the teacher for the number
Discipulus quaerit utrum tria sint an quattuor = The student asks whether it is three or four
Magister discipulō respondet numerum esse quattuor = The teacher answers the student that the number is four
