Cibus et Mēnsa (Food and Table) = Cibus est necessārius — “Food is necessary” — proverb
Coqua placentam parat = The cook prepares a cake
Coqua placentam parātam in furnō pōnit et coquit = The cook puts the prepared cake in the oven and cooks it
Puer placentam coctam edit = The boy eats the cooked cake
Stephanus cum patre mēnsam instruit = Stephanus sets the table with his father
Tiberia aululam in mēnsā instructā pōnit = Tiberia puts the pot on the prepared table
Tiberia iuxtā aululam positam catīnōs adpōnit = Tiberia places dishes next to the pot
