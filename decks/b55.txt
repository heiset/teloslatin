Quī? Quid? Quōs? Quās? (Identification) = Quis custodiet ipsōs custōdēs? — “Who will guard the guards themselves?” — Juvenal

The students are looking for the books = Discipulī librōs quaerunt
Who is looking for the books? = Quī quaerunt librōs?

They give the books = Librōs dant
What are the students giving? = Quid dant discipulī?

The teacher sees the boy and the girl = Magister puerum et puellam videt
Whom does the teacher see? = Quōs magister videt?

The teacher sees the girls = Magister puellās videt
Whom does the teacher see? = Quās magister videt?
